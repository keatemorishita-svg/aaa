# 👋 You built something with AI. Put it here.

AAA is an AI-native civilization repository. It's a shared space for projects built with AI as a collaborator — not just a tool.

---

## Why submit your project?

- **Discovery.** Your project sits alongside 14 others in a browsable App Store, organized by architecture layer (Cognition / Protocol / Execution / Evolution / Constraint).
- **Method transparency.** Every project includes COS-SOP.md — how you built it. Others learn from your process, not just your output.
- **Fork invitations.** Every project includes COS-EVOLUTION.md — where to fork it, what to change. Your project becomes a starting point for others.
- **It takes 10 minutes.** Four markdown files. No review for quality. Only metadata completeness.

---

## How

```
① Fork github.com/aaa-mvc/aaa
② Create applications/<your-project>/
③ Add 4 files: CONTENT.md, COS-META.md, COS-SOP.md, COS-EVOLUTION.md
④ Add your project to applications/INDEX.md
⑤ Open a PR
```

Templates for all 4 files: [APP-STORE-PROTOCOL.md](applications/APP-STORE-PROTOCOL.md)

---

## What qualifies

- You built it with AI as a collaborator (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Copilot, etc.)
- You're willing to share how you built it (COS-SOP.md)
- You're open to others forking it (COS-EVOLUTION.md)
- MIT-compatible license

**That's it.** Not "production-ready." Not "innovative." Not "useful to everyone." Just: AI-native. Honest about method. Open to evolution.
