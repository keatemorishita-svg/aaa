# Brand — Logo Concept

## Core Visual Concept

> A recursive network that continuously forks and merges.

---

## Visual Elements

### Primary Symbol
A circular/network-topology mark composed of:
- **Nodes** — points of divergence and convergence
- **Forks** — branching paths outward
- **Loops** — recursive feedback paths
- **Merge points** — paths flowing back together

### Morphology Options

1. **Ring of Evolution** — a circular network where nodes fork outward and loop back inward
2. **Tree of Divergence** — a branching tree structure showing fork paths
3. **Moebius Adaptation** — a continuous surface where inside becomes outside

### Recommended Direction
The ring/network form — it conveys both openness (no boundary edge) and recursion (paths return).

---

## Logo One-Liner

> A system that evolves itself through recursion.

---

## Usage

- Primary: dark backgrounds (#0B0F19)
- Inverted: light backgrounds
- Monochrome: single-color variants
- Animation: nodes pulsing, paths flowing
