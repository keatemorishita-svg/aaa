# Builder OS (ABA)

> Follow builders, not influencers — 6 AI personas analyze daily news into structured knowledge.

**Repo**: [keatemorishita-svg/aba](https://github.com/keatemorishita-svg/aba)

---

## What it does

Builder OS is an Obsidian integration tool that transforms daily AI industry news into a structured knowledge network. It uses six distinct "cognitive models" based on prominent AI figures (Dario Amodei, Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Demis Hassabis, Andrej Karpathy, Aravind Srinivas) to analyze the same set of news — surfacing where these perspectives converge or diverge.

## Key features

- **6 Builder Personas**: Each simulates a distinct AI leader's thinking style
- **Consensus Detection**: 3+ simulated builders independently reaching the same conclusion → flagged. 4+ → "approaching industry consensus"
- **Multi-LLM Support**: DeepSeek (default), OpenAI, Anthropic, or custom endpoints
- **Bilingual Output**: Chinese, English, or both
- **Obsidian-Native**: Produces hierarchical vault with daily notes, digests, atomic notes, reflection questions, MOC

## AAA Mapping

Builder OS is a **Cognition Layer** application — it structures raw information into knowledge using distributed perspective-taking (a form of Fork → Experiment → Selection applied to news analysis).
