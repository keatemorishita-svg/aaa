# Show · 朋友圈文案

> AI WeChat Moments copy generator — 3-axis customization × 4 tones.

**Repo**: [keatemorishita-svg/show](https://github.com/keatemorishita-svg/show)

---

## What it does

An AI-powered tool for generating WeChat Moments (朋友圈) posts. Users select from "4 personas × 4 scenarios × 4 purposes × 4 rhythms" to produce posts that match their personal voice and context.

## Key features

- **Multi-dimensional customization**: Persona × Scenario × Purpose × Rhythm × Tone
- **Dual AI backend**: DeepSeek and OpenAI compatible
- **Zero dependencies, single-command setup**: `pip install -r requirements.txt && python server.py`
- **Local server**: Runs on port 5002
- **MIT Licensed**

## AAA Mapping

Show is an **Execution Layer** application — it generates social media copy from structured parameters. The persona/scenario/purpose/rhythm matrix is a mini protocol for personal expression.
